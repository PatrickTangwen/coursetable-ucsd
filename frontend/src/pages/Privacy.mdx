# SunGrid privacy notice

_Last updated July 17, 2026_

SunGrid is a UCSD course discovery and schedule-planning service. This notice
describes the information the service uses to provide its public catalog,
Worksheet, and optional account features.

## Information SunGrid uses

- **Public use.** You can browse the catalog and plan a signed-out Worksheet
  without providing an email address. Signed-out Worksheet data is stored in
  your browser.
- **Account use.** If you sign in, SunGrid uses your verified `@ucsd.edu` email
  address and an internal account identifier to authenticate you and associate
  account-owned data such as Saved Worksheets and Saved Searches with you.
- **Verification delivery.** SunGrid sends a one-time verification message to
  the email address you provide. A short-lived delivery-audit record may retain
  the addressed email and delivery outcome for support and abuse prevention; it
  does not contain the verification code, email body, session, or provider
  credential.
- **Technical data.** The hosting, security, and error-monitoring systems may
  process ordinary request and device information needed to operate and protect
  the service.

SunGrid does not use third-party social-login account data for UCSD sign-in.

## How information is used

Information is used only to operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve SunGrid;
authenticate UCSD users; deliver verification messages; and persist the account
features a signed-in user chooses to use. SunGrid does not sell personal
information or use it for third-party advertising.

## Service providers and storage

SunGrid relies on infrastructure and delivery providers to host the application,
store account and session data, send verification messages, and monitor errors.
Those providers process information on SunGrid's behalf as needed to provide
their services. Course catalog data comes from public UCSD sources and is not
linked to an individual user's account.

## Retention and choices

Verification-delivery audit records are designed to expire after seven days.
Hosted login sessions have a fixed thirty-day lifetime. Account-owned planning
data remains until it is deleted through the service or removed in response to a
maintainer-supported request. You can remove signed-out Worksheet data by
clearing this site's browser storage.

## Security

SunGrid uses access controls and environment separation intended to protect
account and operational data. No internet service can guarantee absolute
security, so please report a suspected security or privacy problem promptly.

## Questions and requests

To report a privacy concern or request access to or deletion of account data,
open a private maintainer contact if one is available. Otherwise, create a
[GitHub issue](https://github.com/PatrickTangwen/coursetable-ucsd/issues/new)
without including sensitive personal information; a maintainer can arrange a
private follow-up.
