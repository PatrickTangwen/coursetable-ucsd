## Privacy at a Glance (#at-a-glance)

SunGrid is designed to collect only the information reasonably necessary to provide account access, save worksheets, operate the website, investigate technical problems, prevent abuse, and improve the service.

In particular:

- A school email address is required to sign in to SunGrid.
- SunGrid uses passwordless authentication.
- A temporary six-digit login code is sent to the school email address provided by the user.
- Users do not create or maintain a password specifically for SunGrid.
- SunGrid does not request or store your school email password.
- Courses, sections, worksheets, and related planning preferences may be stored so they can be restored after you sign in.
- Limited technical information may be recorded automatically for security, troubleshooting, and website operation.
- SunGrid does not use your login to access your school email inbox or official university records.
- SunGrid does not sell or rent personal information.
- School email addresses are not used for unrelated promotional marketing without consent.
- You should not submit passwords, student identification numbers, enrollment codes, financial information, or other sensitive personal information.

This summary is provided for convenience. The full Privacy Policy below controls.

## 1. Scope of This Privacy Policy (#scope)

This Privacy Policy applies to information processed through:

- The SunGrid website
- The SunGrid login and account system
- Saved worksheets and course-planning features
- Course-issue reports and feedback forms
- Related services operated by SunGrid

This Privacy Policy does not apply to services operated independently by third parties, including:

- UC San Diego websites or systems
- The Triton Student System
- University authentication or email systems
- External course catalogs
- Department websites
- Third-party hosting providers
- Third-party authentication providers
- Third-party analytics or monitoring providers
- Third-party feedback services
- Other websites linked from SunGrid

Those third-party services maintain their own terms, privacy policies, and security practices.

## 2. Information We Collect (#information-we-collect)

### 2.1 Account information

When you sign in to SunGrid, we may collect or process:

- Your school email address
- A user or account identifier associated with your email address
- The domain associated with your school email address
- Authentication status
- Login and verification timestamps
- Session information
- Limited technical information related to login attempts

Your school email address is used primarily to:

- Send a temporary six-digit login code
- Verify that you currently have access to the email address
- Identify and authenticate your SunGrid account
- Associate saved worksheets with your account
- Prevent spam, abuse, and unauthorized access
- Send essential account, privacy, or security communications where necessary

SunGrid does not use your school email address to access:

- Your school email inbox
- Your email messages or attachments
- Your university account password
- Your official student record
- Your grades
- Your tuition or billing information
- Your official enrollment status
- Your university files or documents

### 2.2 Passwordless authentication and six-digit login codes

SunGrid uses passwordless email authentication.

To sign in, you provide your school email address. SunGrid or its authentication provider sends a temporary six-digit login code to that email address. You then enter the code on SunGrid to confirm that you have access to the email account.

You do not create a password for SunGrid.

SunGrid does not request, receive, or intentionally store:

- Your school email password
- Your university account password
- A separate SunGrid password
- Access credentials for the Triton Student System
- Access credentials for another university system
- The contents of your email inbox

The six-digit login code is temporary. It may expire after a limited period, become invalid after use, or become invalid after a new code is requested.

Do not share a login code with another person. Anyone who has access to both your school email account and an active login code may be able to access your SunGrid account.

Authentication and login-email delivery may be handled by **SunGrid and its email delivery provider, Resend**. That provider may process limited information such as:

- Your email address
- Authentication requests
- Login-code delivery status
- IP address
- Browser or device information
- Login timestamps
- Security and abuse-prevention signals

This information is processed as necessary to provide and secure the authentication service.

### 2.3 Saved worksheets and planning information

When you use SunGrid’s planning features, we may store information such as:

- Courses added to a worksheet
- Selected sections
- Selected offering groups
- Worksheet names
- Academic terms
- Course arrangement
- Schedule layout
- Display preferences
- Planning-related settings
- Dates on which worksheets were created or modified
- Other information you voluntarily add to a worksheet

This information is used to save and restore your planning activity after you sign in.

A worksheet is not an official enrollment record. Adding or saving a course or section on SunGrid does not:

- Enroll you in a course
- Add you to a waitlist
- Reserve a seat
- Drop you from a course
- Modify your official schedule
- Update your university student record
- Submit an enrollment request to a university

### 2.4 Feedback and course-issue reports

When you submit feedback or report a course issue, we may collect:

- The content of your report
- The affected course
- The affected section or offering group
- The academic term
- The page or tab from which the report was submitted
- Screenshots or files you voluntarily upload
- Links or supporting context you provide
- Your email address, if you choose to provide it
- The page URL
- Limited technical information associated with the report

SunGrid may use a third-party service, such as Tally, to collect and process feedback submissions.

Do not include any of the following in a feedback submission:

- Passwords
- Six-digit login codes
- Student identification numbers
- Enrollment or authorization codes
- Financial information
- Private academic records
- Government identification
- Other sensitive personal information

### 2.5 Technical and usage information

When you access or use SunGrid, our servers and service providers may automatically process limited technical information, including:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type
- Operating system
- Approximate date and time of access
- Referring page
- Pages or features accessed
- Session identifiers
- Request logs
- Server logs
- Error messages
- Crash reports
- Performance information
- Security-related activity
- Failed or successful login attempts

This information may be used to:

- Operate the website
- Maintain account sessions
- Diagnose errors
- Investigate outages
- Monitor performance
- Protect accounts
- Prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
- Detect unauthorized access
- Improve reliability and functionality

### 2.6 Cookies and browser storage

SunGrid may use cookies, local storage, session storage, or similar browser technologies to:

- Maintain your authenticated session after you enter a valid login code
- Keep you signed in for an appropriate period
- Remember interface preferences
- Restore worksheet state
- Save temporary planning information
- Prevent abuse and unauthorized access
- Diagnose technical problems

These technologies do not contain your school email password because SunGrid does not request or store that password.

Some cookies or browser-storage items may be necessary for core website functionality. Blocking or deleting them may:

- Sign you out
- Prevent worksheets from saving correctly
- Remove stored preferences
- Prevent certain features from working

### 2.7 Information received from service providers

SunGrid may receive limited information from service providers used to operate the website, including:

- Authentication confirmation
- Login-code delivery status
- Hosting and server logs
- Database activity
- Error and performance reports
- Security alerts
- Email-delivery status
- Feedback-form submissions

The exact information received depends on the third-party services used by SunGrid.
